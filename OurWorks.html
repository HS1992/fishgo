OUR WORK

OUR WORK IN ACTION

We’re working in cooperation to make the people aware about the climate change and it’s impacts so that whatever activities that are leading to these impacts through the anthropogenic activities, we can minimise them for the betterment of our future as well as for our planet also.

We’re making fisheries work better for smallholder fish farmers, many of them women, so they can adopt to climate change and achieve a good standard of living communities.

The combination of our deep understanding in these areas gives us the ability to work effectively with others to make the changes that can deliver more sustainable agriculture, better food security and livelihood for vulnerable fish farming.

We are continuously working to build our profile and influence on regenerative fish farming, developing additional large scale partnership.

We works to aims to develop our niche in energy for fisheries that supports smallholder communities and sustainable rural revitalisation.

Providing the potential to transform the lives of millions of farming families struggling to adopt to a changing climate.
